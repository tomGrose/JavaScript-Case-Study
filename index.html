A little about internet security.

Phishing Avoidance

Any sort of link that takes you to a site that wants you to provide personal information is most likely unsafe. Stay within sites that require you to give personel info.

Review Sites Security Settings

Review social media websites privacy settings. Make sure they are set to keep your information safe

Use a VPN

Using a VPN secures all the data you send and recieve by encrypting it, this way no one can steal your information.
